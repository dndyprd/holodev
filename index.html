Frequently Asked Question FAQ

Some questions that prospective students often ask us regarding the game dev courses that we offer. Still have questions? Chat our team!

Contact Us
There any prerequisites before registering?

No, all our programs are designed for varying skill levels. Beginners can start from scratch without any coding background. The Advanced package for those who already know programming logic.

How long are the programs?

The learning duration varies depending on the package you choose. The Junior or Beginner package will take 3-6 months. The Intermediate/Pro package will take 6-12 months (including portfolio development).

Are courses online or offline?

Both! You can choose either learning method. You can study offline in over 25 cities across Indonesia. However, you can also learn online through live tutoring and recorded modules.

Is there access to materials after graduation?

Yes! Lifetime access to modules, curriculum updates, and the alumni network on Discord.

What PC/laptop specifications are required?

Minimum specifications for Junior or Beginner packages require a standard PC/Laptop (4GB RAM). For pro packages require a dedicated GPU for Unreal Engine 5/Blender.

Can we try a trial class before signing up?

Free 1-day workshop every month! Register via WhatsApp. For more information, send your email using the contact form. Click here
