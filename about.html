About Holodev

Holodev Academy

HoloDev is a game development company and educational platform focused on the world of game development. Founded by a team experienced in the creative and technology industries, HoloDev exists to address the need for quality games and relevant education in today's digital age. We believe that games are not just entertainment, but also powerful means of exploration, expression, and education. At HoloDev, we don't just create games—we build experiences. The games we develop combine strong storytelling elements, engaging gameplay, and captivating visuals. We always follow technological developments and industry trends to ensure every product we produce has high artistic and technical value, for both local and international markets.

In addition, HoloDev also offers various learning programs related to the world of gaming. These range from basic classes for beginners, intensive training like bootcamps, to real-life project mentoring for participants looking to build a professional portfolio. All classes are designed to be easy-to-follow, based on hands-on practice, and guided by mentors who are competent in their fields. This way, anyone—whether just starting out or looking to improve their skills—can learn comfortably and effectively. HoloDev exists not only as a development studio, but also as a place for the growth of the Indonesian game creator community. We encourage collaboration, innovation, and a spirit of mutual support among learners, developers, and industry players. Through this comprehensive approach, HoloDev is committed to building a healthy, inclusive local gaming ecosystem that is ready to compete globally.
